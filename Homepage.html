CAFFEINE INTAKE AND MULTIDIMENSIONAL HEALTH OUTCOMES

Quantifying the associations between coffee-related caffeine exposure and blood pressure, sleep, diabetes, and cardiovascular biomarkers in U.S. adults.

Explore the Project

Caffeine, Coffee, and Health

We analyze nationally-representative NHANES data to investigate how daily caffeine intake relates to blood pressure, sleep duration, diabetes biomarkers, and cardiovascular risk profiles among U.S. adults.

  • Cross-sectional analysis using 2021–2023 NHANES.
  • Focus on coffee-derived caffeine exposure.
  • Regression models adjusting for key confounders.
